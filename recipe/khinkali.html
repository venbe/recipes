Khinkali recipe

Khinkali (Georgian: ხინკალი) is a very popular Georgian dumpling made of twisted knobs of dough, stuffed with meat and spices. It is considered to be one of the national dishes of Georgia.

Different regions of Georgia make khinkali with different fillings. The most popular filling is a pork/beef mix. In the mountains, khinkali is often made with a lamb filling

Fillings can also include Imeretian cheese mixed with cottage cheese; mushrooms; and mashed potato. City versions include kalakuri khinkali (with thinly chopped parsley) and khevsuruli khinkali (without parsley)

ingredients !

  • flour
  • eggs
  • ground beef
  • pork
  • red pepper
  • onion

Steps

  1. Add 1.1 kilo of flour to a mixing bowl. Make a depression in the middle of the flour and add the eggs.
  2. Add 450 ml of warm water.
  3. Mix the ingredients from the middle of the bowl until all of the flour is mixed.
  4. Divide the dough into two pieces.
